# A9. Discover Privacy Technique Used Online

## Chosen Online Privacy Technique: VPN (Virtual Private Network)

A VPN works by routing a devices internet connection through a private channel; rather than transmitting on its usual internet service provider. Essentially the VPN acts as a middle man that connects the user of the device online while encrypting personal information such as their location. Using a VPN protects your browsing history while connected to public networks and keeps your activity private from third parties.  [1]  


*An example of a VPN is NortonVPN, provided by the antivirus software company, Norton.*
