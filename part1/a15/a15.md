## 5 Recent Security Events

1) Louvre Jewel Heist October 2025 

The Louvre in Paris was robbed by two individuals who gained entry via an extendable ladder, forced entry into the holding gallery of encased jewellery. The robbers had stolen $156.6 million worth of historical jewellery in just a few moments and while escaping on motorcycles[1]

2) Victorian Department of Education January 2026

A significant data breach affecting more than 1700 schools.Encrypted credentials of former and current students, names and addresses was accessed without authorisation by a third party, the access point linked to an unsecure school network 
[2]

3) Hacking of Smile Team Orthadontics in March 2026

Staff listings,home addresses,medical certifcates of staff from various clinics of Smile Team Orthadontics was stolen and was posted by the SafePay ransomware group on its dark net site. [3]

4) University Of Sydney Hacked in 2025

Hackers accessed and transferred data via the university's online code libray; stealing 13,000 individuals records belonging to students,donors and staff [4]

5) youX in 2025

An Australian finance technology company which confirmed a databreach in which hackers have compromised 141 GB of data;  affecting 600k loan applications in the system [5]
