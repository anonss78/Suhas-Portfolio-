# A12. Discover 5 unique offline security tools

## 5 Unique Offline Security Tools:

1. **USB Data Blocker**  
    A USB data blocker prevents data from being transferred through a USB cable such as when charging your phone in public places like the library to prevent data loss.


    ***A YouTube link that demonstrates how USB datablockers work: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mHHueCRhENk***


2. **Car Key Fob**  

    A car key fob that locks, unlocks and starts your vehicle without the use of a physical key. In most cases, the vehicle will not start unless the fob is located nearby. 

3. **CCTV Security Camera (Offline Recording)**  

    A CCTV camera records video footage locally while being offline. The recorded video is then saved to an SD card or memory storage for later viewing to monitor activity and theft.

4. **Paper Shredder Used in law firms and banks**  

    A paper shredder shreds documents so others cannot read and piece together sensitive information. For example, it can be used to safely dispose of bank statements and legal documents.

5. **Safe (Lockbox)**  
    Used to store valuable items and important documents safely. Can be used at home to store passports,valuables and documents.
